About This Project

Letters to the Future" is a platform that allows users to write letters to their future selves or to the world, to be delivered in 1 , 5 , 10 years, or even 2050. This encourages deep reflection on personal goals and the long-term impact of today’s actions. The letters can be private, sent only to the user’s email, or public, shared with the community to inspire others and foster collective responsibility.

After logging in, users can view their own sustainability values alongside the global average. This feature provides valuable feedback to inspire positive changes and encourages users to reflect on their impact on the planet.

The quiz is a core feature of the platform, offering a random set of 20 sustainability-related questions every time. It’s a fun and engaging way to test users' knowledge on environmental topics, with badges awarded based on performance. The quiz not only educates but also motivates users to learn more about sustainable practices.

On the homepage , users can access general information about sustainability, including facts and tips to help them make informed decisions. This section is designed to increase awareness and encourage sustainable behaviors in everyday life.
